## Weapon Proficiencies
The following weapon proficiencies are not yet implemented.

### Axe  
- Battleaxe: First hit vs. shielded opponent can force shield drop/unequip

### Polearm
- Pike: Rush ability  
- Halberd: Opportunity attacks can knock prone
- Spear/Trident: Extra attack on reaction
- Special opportunity attacks when enemies enter range or attack others

### Blunt Weapon  
- Disabling Blow (attack at -3, special effects)

### Small Sword
- Bonus DEX damage vs. unarmored/lightly armored
- Shortsword: Extra attack if all main hand attacks hit

### Crossbow  
- No penalty for melee attacks  
- Heavy crossbow: Half damage vs. armor  

### Sword
- Longsword: Piercing damage, extra DEX vs. unarmored
- Scimitar: Extra attack on reaction
