# Metamagic Options

### Empowered Spell
This metamagic feature allows you to spend 2 sorcery points to roll the damage dice of your next spell with advantage.

### Distant Spell
This metamagic feature allows you to spend 1 sorcery point to double the range of your next spell, if the spell has a range of touch (5ft) its range is instead increased to 30ft.

### Extended Spell
This metamagic feature allows you to spend 1 sorcery point to double the duration of any conditions your next spell applies.

### Quickened Spell
This metamagic feature allows you to spend 1 sorcery points to halve the cast time of your next spell.

### Transmute Spells
This metamagic feature allows you to spend 2 sorcery points so that the damage from your spells is changed to the chosen element of: acid, cold, fire, lighting, poison, thunder. This metamagic option lasts indefinitely.
